Packed Red Blood Cells

What is it? Blood collected straight from the donor into a blood bag and mixed with an anticoagulant...

Who can donate? You need to be 18-65 years old...

User For? Correction of severe anemia in a number of conditions...

Lasts For? Red cells can be stored for 42 days...

How long does it take to donate? 15-30 minutes...

How often can I donate? Male donors can donate again after 90 days...

Plasma

What is it? Plasma is the yellowish liquid component...

Who can donate? Healthy individuals aged 18-65...

User For? Plasma is used for patients with clotting disorders...

Lasts For? Plasma can be stored for up to one year...

How long does it take to donate? Plasma donation typically takes 45-60 minutes...

How often can I donate? Plasma donation is possible every 28 days...

Platelet Donation

What is it? Platelet donation involves the collection of platelets...

Who can donate? Donors need to be healthy, aged 18-65...

User For? Platelet donation is used for patients with bleeding disorders...

Lasts For? Platelets can be stored for only 5-7 days...

How long does it take to donate? Platelet donation can take up to 2 hours...

How often can I donate? Platelets can be donated once every 7 days...
